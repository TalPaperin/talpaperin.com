What is the Sales Doctor?

The Sales Doctor is a free AI sales diagnosis tool for B2B founders and revenue leaders. Instead of a generic chatbot answer, it diagnoses why your sales have stalled using the same frameworks fractional CRO Tal Paperin uses after rebuilding 30-plus B2B sales organizations and managing $20M in ARR. You describe the situation, it tells you the most likely root cause, what is probably going on under the surface, and what to check first this week.

What it diagnoses

  • A pipeline that looks full but never closes
  • Deals that go quiet after a great demo
  • A third VP of Sales gone in 18 months
  • Founder-led sales that will not scale past the founder
  • Low win rates, long sales cycles, and chronic discounting
  • The wrong ICP, weak outbound, and stalled US market entry

Why it is not generic

Most AI tools give textbook advice. This one reasons the way an operator does: sales problems are almost always system problems, not people problems. A motion that only works when the founder is in the room is not a motion. Activity is not progress. The Sales Doctor names the uncomfortable thing plainly, then tells you whether it is fixable and how fast.
