  <p>The guy took a selfie (probably to use on Social Media) - and this picture of him doing so ended up being Viral.</p> 
  <p>The lesson I re-learned yet again is very simple. While doing your job - and it doesn't matter what the job is - remember the following:</p> 
  <p>Whatever you are doing. Whatever you are selling / producing / manufacturing / supplying - and the way you do so - might AND WILL end up on Social Media.</p> 
  <p>People will read and hear and see all about your "business persona" - and the ripples will go far and wide.</p>
