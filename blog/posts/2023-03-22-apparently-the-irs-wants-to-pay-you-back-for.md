Apparently, the IRS wants to pay you back for keeping your employees on payroll during COVID. We're talking about up to $26K PER EMPLOYEE!

That's called ERC.

The Employee Retention Tax Credit (known as ERC or ERTC) is a stimulus program authorized under the CARES Act. It pays sizable refunds to businesses that kept employees on payroll during and beyond the COVID-19 pandemic.
