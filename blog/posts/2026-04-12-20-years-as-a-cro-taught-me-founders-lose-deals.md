I've watched brilliant founders lose deals they should have closed.

I've watched great reps underperform because nobody built them a system.

I've watched startups burn hundreds of thousands on sales leadership that took months to ramp and never delivered.

For the last decade I've been consulting those exact companies.

The problem was always the same.

They didn't know how to sell. They didn't know where to sell. They didn't know who to sell to.

I got tired of watching it happen.

So I built something about it.
