Manufactured by Eviation Aircraft Ltd - an Israeli startup, this plane, called Alice, can carry up to nine passengers and has a range of up to 650 miles and cruising speed of 276 mph.

The aircraft is designed for short-haul commuter flights and costs about $4 million, much cheaper than conventional jet-powered planes The plane has three MagniX engines, which can produce 900 kilowatts of power.
