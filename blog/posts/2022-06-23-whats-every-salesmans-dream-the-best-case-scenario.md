<div><p>What’s every Salesman’s dream? The best-case scenario?</p><p>When a client with a clear need, a burning, immediate and costly problem ,  and with money, authority and desire to solve it - comes to HIM! But is that enough?</p><p>Several large, multinational, companies (such as Philips and Strauss) participated in a seminar in Israel, during which they presented quite a few problems they are currently facing ,  mostly in the Industry 4.0 and Advanced manufacturing areas (but not only).</p><p>Those can be easily solved by IoT and SaaS (and a few tweaks to the actual process).</p><p>Being in Sales means being able to match a solution to a problem. For that, you need to have the ability to analyze the issues by conducting discovery.</p><p>Having a willing partner on the other side is quite helpful…. But it’s not enough.</p><p>Remember that it is all about THEM ,  and not you and your product.</p><p>Having the ability to ask the right questions ,  and listen carefully to the answers.</p><p>Having the skill and expertise to TALK and have a conversation ,  a well-structured, purposeful one.</p><p>THOSE should be enough.</p></div>
