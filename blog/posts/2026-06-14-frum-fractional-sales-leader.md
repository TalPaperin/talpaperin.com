I am a frum, shomer Shabbos Yid from Eretz Yisrael, and I have built and run sales for B2B companies on four continents. When I work with a frum business, you do not have to translate your world for me. I already live in it.

## What a fractional sales leader is

A fractional sales leader is a senior revenue boss you bring in part time instead of paying a full VP of Sales a fortune. I own the strategy, the team, the pipeline, the forecast and the number, hands-on, from diagnosis to closing. Not a deck. The actual work.

For a frum business owner that means you get VP-level sales leadership without a six-figure hire, and you get it from someone who shares your values.

## The frum part is not a gimmick

I will not pick up on Shabbos. I will not push a meeting on Yom Tov. I build the work around the Jewish calendar without being asked, because it is my calendar too. A handshake with a fellow Yid means something to me, and discretion in a close kehilla is something I take seriously.

## The work is still hard revenue

Sharing a value system does not close deals. Skill does. I build go-to-market from zero, hire and train salespeople, open new markets, recruit distributors, and run complex B2B deals. Same operator for everyone. The difference for you is that you also get a leader who understands the heimishe world you operate in.

## Who this is for

Frum business owners who are tired of carrying sales alone, tired of VP hires that did not move the number, and who want someone in their corner who shares their values and still knows how to sell in the real world.
