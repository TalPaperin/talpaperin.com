Business development is not networking and it is not luck. ## What business development actually means

Business development is where most frum businesses leave the most money on the table. They know their own kehilla cold and have no system for everything beyond it. I find the partners and markets worth pursuing, build the relationships on purpose, and turn reach into signed, repeatable revenue.

## What I do

- Identify the partners, channels and markets actually worth your time
- Recruit and manage distributors, resellers and referral partners
- Open new verticals and new geographies, including outside the kehilla
- Build the outreach and the relationships that turn into real deals
- Run complex B2B and B2G deals from first contact to close

## A frum operator who shares your world

I am a frum, shomer Shabbos Yid from Eretz Yisrael. No calls, meetings or messages on Shabbos or Yom Tov, ever. Straight talk, discretion in a close kehilla, and the emes about where your revenue is leaking, then the work to fix it.
