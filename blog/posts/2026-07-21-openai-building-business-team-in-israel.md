OpenAI is building its first real business presence in Israel. Not a development center. Business development, account management and direct work with Israeli companies.

That is the tell.

OpenAI just admitted something most of the world already knows: Israel is not only good at building AI. We are among the best in the world at turning it into business.

That is the real story.

Israel is not a huge market. We do not have the largest population, the biggest budgets, or our own dominant foundation model. And yet OpenAI wants people on the ground here, because Israeli companies adopt new technology fast, challenge it, build on it, and find ways to make money from it.

According to the reporting, Israel ranks first in the world in Claude usage per capita among working professionals. We also rank near the top in AI talent and in private investment. That combination is the whole point. Technology on its own is useless. What matters is whether a company can turn it into better products, faster execution and real revenue.

## This is exactly why I can sit in Israel and run sales for American companies

Most of my clients are US-based. I join their leadership meetings, review their pipeline, rebuild their sales process, [hire and train the salespeople](/services/sales-team-building), manage the team, challenge the forecast and help close deals. I work US hours, and nobody cares that I am thousands of miles away.

They care whether I can solve the problem.

For years, companies hired based on location. The smarter ones now hire based on results.

## The same lesson applies to AI, and to sales

Buying a tool does not make a company innovative. Hiring another salesperson does not fix a broken sales organization. Adding another dashboard does not create revenue.

The companies that win are the ones that combine the technology with real business judgment, proper execution, and people who actually know what they are doing.

That is what Israel is very good at. And that is exactly what I do for US companies as a business consultant and [fractional CRO based in Israel](/fractional-cro-israel): I walk into a situation where sales are underperforming, find what is actually broken, and fix it.

Based in Israel. Working with American companies. Focused on results.
