Just in case you DON'T have 3 minutes to listen to Shark Tank's Robert Herjavec (and you SHOULD!), here they are:

1. Look for a problem - and solve it! 2. Ask a PAYING customer. 3. Pay the rent! 4. Narrow your focus! 5. Move faster and learn how to say no!

The first point is my favorite. What's yours?
