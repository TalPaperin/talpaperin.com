The Netherlands is one of the easiest, smartest entry points into continental Europe, if you sell the way the Dutch actually buy. I have built, hired, trained and managed sales teams in the Netherlands, more than once and for several companies, and here is how to do it right.

## Is the Netherlands actually your market?

Small country, outsized commercial reach. English is widely spoken, the business culture is open, and it is a natural hub into the rest of Europe. That makes it a strong first or second European market. I help you judge the fit, then build it.

## How Dutch buyers really buy

The Dutch are direct, pragmatic and allergic to hype. They will tell you exactly what they think, expect you to do the same, and respect a clear, honest, well-priced proposition over a slick pitch. They value efficiency and fairness, decide relatively quickly for Europe, and trust vendors who are straight with them. Overselling backfires fast.

## What I build and run in the Netherlands

- A sales team, hired, trained and managed (English-capable, local where it counts)
- Positioning built for a direct, no-hype Dutch buyer
- A local entity when customers or hiring require it
- Tech support and customer service operations
- The CRM, pipeline, forecast and accountability

## How I would open the Netherlands

Be direct, be honest, and price fairly. Lead with substance, not theatre, and use the Netherlands as a base into the wider EU. I run it hands-on, in the seat.
