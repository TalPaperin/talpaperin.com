I hope I've managed to bring value to the individuals and companies I've met with yesterday.

I was very impressed with The Israel Center for Entrepreneurship and their coworking space in Jerusalem! They have everyone from a company worth hundreds of millions of dollars to-up-and-coming freelancers. They are the second largest coworking space in Jerusalem, but sure to be the largest once more people hear about the innovative and supportive ecosystem they've built for startup and entrepreneurs for religious people in Jerusalem. If you are interested in learning more you can contact them through their page or reach out to Yaakov Hudson.
