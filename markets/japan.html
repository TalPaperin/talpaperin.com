Building a Sales Operation in Japan

Japan is one of the hardest markets to crack and one of the most loyal once you do. I have built and managed sales teams in Japan, and here is how to earn your way in.

Is Japan actually your market?

Japan is large, wealthy and quality-obsessed, and it rewards patience like nowhere else. But it is slow, relationship-driven and intolerant of shortcuts. I help you judge whether you have the product and the patience for Japan, then build it properly.

How Japanese buyers really buy

Japanese buyers buy trust, quality and certainty, and they buy through consensus, not a single decision-maker. The decision moves through a group process, so the real work is building agreement across the organization, quietly, over time. Relationships and reliability matter more than a slick pitch, and a rushed, aggressive approach ends the conversation. Once you are trusted, you are a long-term partner.

What I build and run in Japan

  • A Japanese sales team, hired, trained and managed
  • Positioning and materials built for quality, trust and consensus buying
  • A local entity and the right partners when the market requires it
  • Tech support and customer service to Japanese reliability standards
  • The CRM, pipeline, forecast and accountability

How I would open Japan

Invest in relationships and local people, respect the consensus process, and never rush it. Quality and follow-through are everything. I do this hands-on, in the seat, with the patience the market demands.
