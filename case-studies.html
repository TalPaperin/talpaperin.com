Case Studies

Brought in to build the engine, not advise on it.

Real companies, real problems, real ownership. Here is what I have built and fixed, across startups, IoT, manufacturing, medical devices, retail and SaaS, on four continents.

KanduAI

AI SaaS startup, Fractional VP of Sales

An early-stage AI startup with no sales engine. They needed someone to build it from day one, then it survived a full product pivot.

  • Owned go-to-market end to end as fractional VP of Sales from day one
  • Defined and validated the ICP, positioning and messaging
  • Built the outbound playbook and motion from zero
  • Hired, trained and managed the SDR team
  • Relaunched the entire go-to-market through the pivot, twice
ResultA working outbound engine, rebuilt from scratch through a pivot, with speed and precision.

LoneStar Tracking

Founder-led IoT business, Outsourced sales leadership

Strong marketing, but a sales engine that was not converting, and founders with little sales experience.

  • Built the entire sales strategy and motion from scratch
  • Selected and implemented the CRM and the sales stack
  • Built the process, the playbook and the pipeline
  • Recruited, hired and trained the sales team
  • Ran the team and the motion day to day
ResultFull VP-level sales leadership at a fraction of the cost of a hire.

Bacsoft

Israeli IIoT, backed by Japan's SUN Corp, VP of Global Sales

An IIoT company that needed a global sales strategy, a channel network, and the ability to win complex public-sector deals.

  • Owned the global sales strategy
  • Recruited and managed distributors worldwide
  • Ran direct B2B sales of SaaS, hardware and services
  • Led B2G: RFIs, RFQs and complex government projects across the globe
  • Managed marketing and marcom
ResultA global channel and a B2G pipeline built for a company selling complex IIoT internationally.

Palram

Global manufacturer, Eastern Europe market entry

A global manufacturer convinced there was no money in Eastern Europe, so they stayed out.

  • Built the market-entry strategy and milestone plan
  • Mapped the channel and the real decision-makers on the ground
  • Led every negotiation personally
  • Signed multiple distributors and retail chains across the region
ResultProduct on shelves in a market the company had written off, proving there was real money in Eastern Europe.
